About Us

At Aliya Pink Tours, we believe travel should be personal, meaningful, and rooted in genuine local connection. Founded in 2015 by our Chairman Mr Sivakumar, a seasoned tourist chauffeur guide with over 20 years of hands-on experience, our journey began with a simple yet powerful mission: to craft unforgettable travel experiences that reflect the true spirit of Sri Lanka.

Our chairman’s deep understanding of the island, its people, its places, and its many hidden gems, comes from decades spent guiding travellers from around the world. Fluent in multiple languages and with an innate understanding of what visitors truly want (and what they don’t), he built Aliya Pink Tours from the ground up as a heartfelt expression of Sri Lankan hospitality.

Our tours go beyond checklists and tourist trails. Every itinerary is designed with care, combining well-loved highlights with authentic local encounters. We don’t believe in one size fits all travel. Instead, we listen, we adapt, and we create journeys that match your dreams, whether you're seeking culture, nature, relaxation, or a bit of everything.

But our passion for experience-making doesn’t stop at travel. We also specialize in event coordination, curating magical destination weddings and intimate celebrations across Sri Lanka. From breathtaking beach ceremonies to elegant mountain retreats, we handle every detail with care, allowing you to focus on making memories.

At Aliya Pink Tours, we’re more than a tour agent, we’re storytellers, matchmakers of moments, and proud ambassadors of Sri Lankan warmth. If you’re looking for a travel partner who understands both the art of the journey and the beauty of a well-celebrated milestone, we’d love to hear from you.

Let’s create something unforgettable together.
