13-Day Cultural Triangle Itinerary

Day 1: Travel to Negombo after arriving in Colombo:

Your guide will meet you when you arrive and take you to Negombo, a seaside town near Colombo. Relax & Leisure: After a long travel, unwind at your hotel by the sea.

  • Arrival at Colombo airport
  • Check-in and rest at the hotel

Day 2: Negombo to Anuradhapura-Anuradhapura Tour:

Drive to Anuradhapura, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of Sri Lanka's historic capitals, for five hours after breakfast. See the Isurumuniya Temple, Ruwanwelisaya Stupa, and Sri Maha Bodhi Tree. Discover Ancient Ruins: Discover the remarkably intact remnants of historic mansions, temples, and monasteries.

  • Visit Anuradhapura
  • Explore The Temples
  • Village Tour In Anuradhapura

Day 3: Anuradhapura to Mihintale & Polonnaruwa-Mihintale Visit:

Take in the important historical site of Mihintale, the place where Buddhism was initially brought to Sri Lanka. In the afternoon, visit Polonnaruwa Ancient City, another UNESCO World Heritage Site, which is well-known for its magnificent archaeological structures, such as Gal Vihara and the historic royal residences.

  • Visit Polonnaruwa
  • Visit All Temples

Day 4:  Polonnaruwa to Sigiriya:

Get to Sigiriya Rock Fortress: It takes two hours to get there. Discover the imposing Sigiriya Rock Fortress, one of the most recognizable sites in Sri Lanka. For sweeping vistas, go to the summit. Discover the history of the palace erected on this enormous rock while seeing the historic frescoes and Lion's Gate.

  • Visit Sigiriya rock fortress
  • Visit Pidhurangala

Day 5: Sigiriya to Dambulla and Kandy:

Dambulla Cave Temple: Visit the Dambulla Cave Temple, which is well-known for its elaborate Buddha sculptures and cave paintings, on your way to Kandy. Kandy Arrival: Proceed to Kandy, Sri Lanka's cultural center.

  • Visit the Dhambulla Cave Temple
  • Visit Kandy

Day 6: Explore Kandy:

Temple of the Tooth: Take a tour of the Temple of the Tooth, a popular pilgrimage destination that is home to a revered relic of Buddha. Explore the charming Kandy Lake and pay a visit to the Royal Botanical Gardens.

  • visit Temples
  • Visit Botanical garden
  • Explore The Kandy Cultural Dance Dance
  • Explore The Local Market

Day 7: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya:

Cultural Visit: Before setting out for Nuwara Eliya, renowned for its colonial architecture and temperate environment, take in the Kandy Cultural Dance Show. Insight into the tea-making process can be gained by taking a picturesque drive through tea farms.

  • Explore The Tea Plantation
  • Explore The Nuwara Eliya Town

Day 8: Exploration of Nuwara Eliya:

Discover Nuwara Eliya Town, also referred to as "Little England," where you may stop by Victoria Park and Lake Gregory. One of the most beautiful waterfalls in Sri Lanka is the Ramboda Waterfall, which is worth a visit.

  • Visit Ramboda Water Fall
  • Visit Gregory Lake

Day 9: Nuwara Eliya to Ella:

Train Ride to Ella: Enjoy one of the world's most breathtaking train trips from Nuwara Eliya to Ella. Discover Ella: Take in the breathtaking views of the surrounding hills from Little Adam's Peak and the Nine Arch Bridge.

  • Visit In the Local Train
  • visit adam's peak
  • Visit Nine Arch Bridge

Day 10: Ella to Yala National Park:

- Morning Exploration: Visit Ella Rock for panoramic views.

- Yala National Park: Drive to Yala National Park in the afternoon for a safari experience. Yala is famous for its wildlife, particularly leopards and elephants.

Day 11: Yala to Galle:

- Galle Fort: Travel to Galle on the southwestern coast of Sri Lanka. Explore the historic Galle Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site with colonial architecture.

- Evening at Leisure: Stroll along the Galle Fort Ramparts and enjoy the ocean breeze.

Day 12: Galle to Colombo:

- Relax & Explore: Spend the morning in Galle, enjoying the beaches and local markets.

- Return to Colombo: Drive to Colombo, Sri Lanka's vibrant capital city, for an afternoon of exploration.

Day 13: Departure:

- Colombo City Tour: Visit Galle Face Green, Independence Square, and the Colombo National Museum.

- Departure: Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your flight home, marking the end of a cultural journey through Sri Lanka.
