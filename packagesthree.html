8-Day Beach Travel Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo – Transfer to Negombo:

Meet your guide when you arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB), and then make your way to Negombo, a seaside town renowned for its beaches and Dutch-era buildings. Leisure Time: Unwind after your trip and check out the canals or seafood markets in the area.

  • Arrival at Colombo airport
  • Check-in and rest at the hotel

Day 2: Negombo to Hikkaduwa:

- Transfer to Hikkaduwa: See the lively seaside town of Hikkaduwa, which is well-known for its surf locations and coral reefs. Diverse marine life may be seen in the Hikkaduwa Coral Sanctuary, where snorkeling is permitted. Beginner surfing classes or free surfing for more seasoned surfers. Relaxing on the beach in the evening while taking in the sunset.

  • Snorkeling
  • Surfing lessons

Day 3: Hikkaduwa to Mirissa:

Transfer to Mirissa: Continue on to Mirissa, which is well-known for its crescent-shaped beach and chances to see whales. Activities: - Whale Watching Tour to see dolphins and blue whales (seasonal: November to April). - For sweeping vistas, go to Coconut Tree Hill. Unwinding on Secret Beach, a remote location perfect for swimming.

Day 4: Mirissa to Trincomalee:

The trip continues to Trincomalee, a port city on the northeast coast renowned for its historical significance and beautiful harbor. Spend your free time exploring the neighborhood or lounging on the beach.

Day 5: Explore Trincomalee:

Activities: - Pigeon Island National Park: Snorkel amid colorful coral reefs and a variety of aquatic life after a quick boat journey to this marine sanctuary. - Koneswaram Temple: Take in the expansive vistas of the Indian Ocean from this ancient Hindu temple situated on Swami Rock. Discover Fort Fredrick, a Portuguese fort constructed in the 17th century that was subsequently held by the British and Dutch. - Pathirakali Amman Temple: Explore this vibrant temple to the goddess Bhadrakali in the Dravidian architecture.

Day 6: Trincomalee to Arugam Bay:

- Transfer to Arugam Bay: Proceed to the relaxed town of Arugam Bay, which is well-liked by surfers. Leisure Time: Explore the neighborhood cafés and shops or unwind on the beach.

Day 7: Arugam Bay Exploration:

Yoga lesson or surf session in the morning. A trip to Kumana National Park to observe animals and birds. A seafood supper or barbecue on the beach in the evening.

  • Breakfast at the hotel
  • Yoga Class / Surf Session
  • Bird Park

Day 8: Arugam Bay to Colombo Via Galle– Departure:

Departure: Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your flight home, marking the end of a cultural journey through Sri Lanka.
