10-Day Tracking Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Colombo & Transfer to Negombo:

Meet your guide when you arrive at Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB), and then make your way to Negombo, a seaside town renowned for its beaches and Dutch-era buildings. Leisure Time: Unwind after your trip and check out the canals or seafood markets in the area.

  • Arrival at colombo airport
  • Check-in and rest at the hotel

Day 2: Negombo to Sigiriya via Dambulla:

See the UNESCO-designated Dambulla Cave Temple, which is well-known for its historic Buddhist figures and fresco murals. Sigiriya Rock fortification: Explore the famous fortification from the 5th century, which is also a UNESCO site, and take in the expansive vistas and paintings.

  • Visit Dambulla Cave Temple
  • Visit Sigiriys Rock Fortress

Day 3: Hike in Ritigala Forest Monastery:

- Ritigala Monastery Ruins: Explore the historic monastery complex tucked away on Ritigala Mountain by starting a strenuous hike through a thick jungle.

- Cultural Insights: Discover the myths surrounding this enigmatic location and its distinctive vegetation.

Day 4: Knuckles Mountain Range Trek:

Knuckles Range: Spend the entire day hiking in this biosphere reserve, which is recognized by UNESCO and is distinguished by its varied ecosystems and rocky peaks. Village Encounters: Visit isolated settlements and engage with the local populace.

Day 5: Kandy to Nuwara Eliya via Tea Plantations:

- Tea Plantation Walk: Explore verdant tea farms and stop by a factory to see how tea is made. Exploration of Nuwara Eliya: Learn about this colonial-era town, sometimes called "Little England," which is renowned for its gardens and temperate temperature.

Day 6: Horton Plains National Park Hike:

- World's End Trail: Trek to the striking cliff that offers sweeping vistas and an 870-meter plummet. Explore Baker's Falls, a charming waterfall tucked away in the park's montane meadows.

Day 7: Train Journey to Ella & Little Adam's Peak Hike:

Take a scenic train ride from Nanu Oya to Ella, which is regarded as one of Asia's most picturesque train trips. Little Adam's top: For breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys and tea plantations, take a leisurely trek to this top.

  • Visit In The Train
  • Hike Little Adams Peak

Day 8: Ella to Galle:

- Nine Arches Bridge: Take in this architectural wonder, a colonial-era viaduct surrounded by rich vegetation. Discover the Galle Fort, a walled city with a fascinating colonial past and a UNESCO World Heritage Site.

  • Visit Beaches In Galle
  • Relax

Day 9: Galle:

Leisure

  • Visit Beaches In Galle
  • Relax

Day 10: Departure:

Departure: Transfer to Bandaranaike International Airport for your flight home, marking the end of a cultural journey through Sri Lanka.
